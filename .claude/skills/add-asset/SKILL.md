## steps
1. delegate to asset-scout
2. find candidates from Kenney, OpenGameArt, Freesound, etc
3. verify license is CC0 or compatible
4. register in apps/web/public/assets/MANIFEST.json with attribution

## deliverable
asset committed with license entry.

## house style
- plain english
- no em dashes
- short sentences
- specific over generic
- if blocked, ask, don't invent
