## steps
1. capture title, severity, repro steps, expected vs actual
2. tag area (frontend, server, llm, etc)
3. assign or queue

## deliverable
a bug entry in docs/decisions/bugs/ with all fields.

## house style
- plain english
- no em dashes
- short sentences
- specific over generic
- if blocked, ask, don't invent
