## steps
1. identify which design templates apply
2. delegate to relevant design agents (creative-director, systems-designer, etc)
3. collect deliverables in docs/design/specs/<feature>/
4. loop until all templates are filled or explicitly waived

## deliverable
a complete design pack in docs/design/specs/<feature>/.

## house style
- plain english
- no em dashes
- short sentences
- specific over generic
- if blocked, ask, don't invent
