## steps
1. read the relevant bible section
2. diff the proposed change against canon
3. if no conflict, confirm and proceed
4. if conflict, route to game-bible-keeper with the diff

## deliverable
either a 'no conflict' verdict or a routed conflict for the bible-keeper.

## house style
- plain english
- no em dashes
- short sentences
- specific over generic
- if blocked, ask, don't invent
